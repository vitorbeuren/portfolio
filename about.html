Who am I?

My name is Vitor Beuren Andrade (24), I am a Brazilian multimedia developer living in Porto, Portugal, and studying philosophy in the University of Porto. It is said that Socrates worked as a mason and the Apostle Paul built tents; following their example, in my turn, I develop media — video, motion graphics, images, courses, graphic material etc.

There is nothing unworthy about working for money — those two caracters I mentioned did that. Even so, I am close to multimedia development since I was a teenager, feeling very "at home" and happy doing my job. Thus, my goal is always to produce the best regarding consistency and quality, always striving for excellency.
