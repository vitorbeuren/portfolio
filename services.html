How Can I Serve You?

E-Learning Authoring

The development of a virtual course can vary from text formatting, or video editing only, to the sum of multiple formats of media put together. As an e-learning developer, one has to be prepared to be a video editor, an audio editor, an image editor, and a course author, to exercise coding skills and pedagogical knowledge. I have been through these fields individually and now use them together for course authoring.

Video Editing

When it comes to video editing, it's not simply a matter of putting together images and audio and pushing a button for the final product. While templates can help, effective video editing requires careful consideration of the intended objective. This includes selecting appropriate materials, determining the right pace, tonality, music, color, and format. Whether it's a course subject or a product advertisement, the ultimate goal is to convey the intended message.

Graphic Design

The field of graphic design is vast and can cater to different requirements. I have had the opportunity to work on multiple projects related to developing visual identities, and every project has been a unique experience, involving the creation of logos, business cards, social media templates and profile media, font and colour selection, and brand guidelines. I consider graphic design to be a comprehensive and personalized process that serves both the client and their customers.

Motion Design

Creating motion graphics can be a demanding task, but the satisfaction of overcoming the challenge is equally rewarding. This process involves breathing life into a brand's visual identity, allowing it to make a stronger impact in video content across various platforms.
